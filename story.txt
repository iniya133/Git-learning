Once upon there was a little girl named red riding hood.
She was called so as she wore a red riding hood.
One day her mother told her to her to give fruits to her sick grandma who lives in the forest.
She packed all the items in a basket and started off to the woods.
On the way , She saw a Wolf .
The Wolf asked her where she was going.
She told him that she was on her way to her grandmother's house.
The Wolf asked her to give something from the basket.
She gave all the things in the basket to the Wolf.
